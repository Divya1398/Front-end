Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This Apple Pie Receipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized apples,peeled and sliced.

Istructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.F(190.C)
  2. In a bowl,mix the ugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar cinnamon mixture.
